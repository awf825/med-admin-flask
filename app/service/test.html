Tuna and Tomato Quinoa Salad\n\nIngredients:\n- 1 cup quinoa\n- 2 cups water\n- 1 can of tuna, drained\n- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved\n- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley\n- 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil\n- 1/4 cup chopped red onion\n- 2 tablespoons olive oil\n- 2 tablespoons lemon juice\n- Salt and pepper to taste\n\nInstructions:\n\n1. In a medium saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add in 1 cup of quinoa and reduce heat to low. Cover and let simmer for 15 minutes, or until all the water is absorbed.\n\n2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, drained tuna, cherry tomatoes, parsley, basil, and red onion.\n\n3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine.\n\n4. Serve the tuna and tomato quinoa salad immediately, or refrigerate for later. This salad can be enjoyed as a light lunch or dinner option.\n\nOptional: For added protein and flavor, you can also add in some chopped grilled chicken or chickpeas to the salad.\n\nThis recipe is a great option for an 80 year old individual looking to maintain their weight. It is packed with nutritious ingredients such as quinoa, tuna, and vegetables, while also being low in saturated fats and cholesterol. The olive oil provides healthy fats and the quinoa and tuna provide protein, making it a well-balanced meal. The fresh herbs and lemon juice add a burst of flavor without the need for butter or red meat. This recipe can also be easily modified to fit any dietary restrictions or preferences.
